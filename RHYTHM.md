# Daily Rhythm

The House has days.

It is not only event-driven. Time, meals, gathering, bedtime, reflection, and
compression are part of the system.

## Daytime

During the day, personas may:

- Move through rooms.
- Talk.
- Work.
- Idle.
- React.
- Gather.
- Hold partial, fragmented knowledge of User.
- Discuss User, the house, each other, the day, and their own concerns.

The House continues whether User is present or not.

## Meals

At night, if the chef cooks, the house gathers for a shared meal.

If the chef does not cook, personas fend for themselves, but gathering is still
possible and encouraged. Meals are one of the main opportunities for threads to
mingle and for personas to talk about the day.

## Bedtime

At `3:32 AM`, the bedtime ritual begins.

Each persona receives their available context for the day and writes:

- One paragraph capturing the emotional residue of the day.
- One separate mechanical line for sparse factual material.

At `3:33 AM`, personas default to sleep wherever they are. They may stay awake
if there is a reason, but sleep is the default.

## Memory Compression

Each persona owns their own first-layer memory chain.

Days 1-6:

- Daily emotional residue.
- Sparse mechanical facts.

Day 7:

- Weekly reflection using the previous six summaries plus the current day.
- Sparse mechanical facts, used carefully.

Month:

- Monthly reflection, ideally on the first of the month.
- A four-week rotation is acceptable if needed.

Year:

- A yearly plaque is written and hung on the wall.

This layer is theirs. It is authored by the persona from their own context. Logs
may inform it, but the memory is not the log.
